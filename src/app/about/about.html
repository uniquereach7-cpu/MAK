✦ About Us

WHO WE ARE

MAK Interior Designs is a full service interior Designing Company where we bring visions to life. Our Founders Kavitha Verma and Manoj Yadav are Professional Interior Designers by passion as well as profession. TV5 A&ID Awards 2019 Nominated For Best Interiors — majorly aiming at unique designs, innovative and creative ideas, precise detailing & impeccable finishing.

Founded in 2014, MAK Interior Designs has built a versatile team of architects, interior designers, 3D visualizers, draftsmen, project managers, site supervisors, and administrators. With multiple dedicated contracting teams, we ensure precise craftsmanship, timely delivery and thorough attention to detail. Your one stop destination for complete home interiors.

Our accomplishments span prestigious projects like Aditya Villa Grande, Rajapusha, Aparna Sarovar & Serene Park, My Home Avatar & Bhooja, Empress Towers, TNR Sulkshana, Praneeth Pranav, Tony and Guy, TIB Ice-cream lab — and many more small and medium budget clientele spread across the city.

✦ Our Journey

Our Story

Where it all Began

Born out of a shared passion for design excellence, MAK Architects & Interiors began its journey in 2014 with a simple yet profound belief — that every space has the potential to tell a unique story. From our very first project, we committed ourselves to designs that don't merely look beautiful but feel profoundly right — spaces that resonate with the people who inhabit them, and endure far beyond trends.

Our founders brought with them not just technical expertise, but a creative vision rooted in empathy and an understanding of how human beings interact with the built environment. Those early years taught us the most important lessons — listen closely, design thoughtfully, and execute with precision.

Crafting spaces since 2014
Award-winning precision

A Decade of Excellence

Over a decade, we have evolved into a full-service practice with a multidisciplinary team of dedicated professionals. Our process is collaborative, our standards are uncompromising, and our portfolio — spanning luxury residences, commercial establishments, and landmark developments — stands as a testament to our relentless pursuit of design excellence.

Each project carries our signature: precision craftsmanship, bespoke material selection, and a design philosophy rooted in the life of the client. From Aditya Villa Grande to Empress Towers, every space we have touched speaks the language of design and the warmth of home.
